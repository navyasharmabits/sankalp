Wings of SANKALP

Functional Divisions of the Union

SANKALP functions through distinct organizational Wings. Each Wing has a clearly defined mandate, operational purpose, and internal structure as listed in the Second Schedule. Wings may be active or inactive depending on operational requirements. Below are the Wings and their defined responsibilities.

General Wing

Every member of the Union is automatically a part of this Wing. Its purpose is the moral, intellectual, civic, and character development of all members. This Wing cannot be dissolved under any circumstance.

Research Wing

Studies topics in deep academic detail and produces formal reports, analyses, and papers for use by the Union.

Cultural Preservation Wing

Records, protects, and promotes indigenous cultural elements of India. Exempt from language-law restrictions and may work in any language needed for cultural preservation.

Implementation Wing

Supports members preparing for competitive civil services examinations. Focuses on study assistance, exam material, and academic preparation support.

Technical Wing

Maintains and manages all technical infrastructure and logistics of the Union. Handles digital systems, operational support, and coordination.

Editorial Wing

Compiles, records, edits, and maintains all reports and written documents of the Union. Produces publications and official records as directed by the Khandādhyaksha.

Outreach Wing

Manages external communication and outreach. Handles social media presence, external interactions, and induction-related publicity.
