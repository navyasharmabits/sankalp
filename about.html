About Sankalp

The Public Administration, General Affairs & History Society of BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus

Who We Are

Sankalp is the official Public Administration, General Affairs and History society of BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. With over 60 members, we work to nurture constitutional literacy, political awareness, administrative knowledge, and an understanding of national issues among students through structured discussions, debates, publications, and policy analysis.

Our Mission

To develop a generation of informed students who understand the functioning of governance, public policy, law, administration and national issues, while encouraging analytical thinking and responsible civic awareness.

What We Do

General Wing Meets

Weekly debate-based discussions where we explore constitutional principles, national issues, administrative systems and contemporary political events.

Research & Publications

The Publications Wing produces well-researched reports, analyses, and position papers on topics from the Third Schedule.

Events & Workshops

We conduct structured lectures, guest sessions, documentary screenings and collaborative academic events focusing on history, public policy, and governance.

Our Structure

The society consists of multiple wings including the General Wing, Publications Wing, History Wing, Operations Wing and more, each supervised by the Executive Office led by the Amatya.
Key bodies include the Mandate Acceptance Committee, Selection Council, and Research divisions, ensuring transparency, accountability and academic depth.

Our Commitment

As the only society on campus dedicated to public administration and national affairs, Sankalp strives to uphold academic integrity, transparency in internal processes, and constitutional literacy among the student body.
