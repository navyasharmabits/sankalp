Wings

Sankalp consists of multiple functional divisions known as Wings. Each Wing has distinct responsibilities and operates with defined mandates to ensure smooth functioning.

  • General Wing: Focuses on debate meets and interactive sessions.
  • Research Wing: Compiles reports and handles analytical documentation.
  • Administration Wing: Coordinates communication and logistics.

About Us

Established with a vision to cultivate informed leadership, Sankalp is the only Public Administration, General Affairs, and History-oriented club on campus. We are committed to shaping a democratic and accountable student environment through structured engagement, academic exploration, and institutional contributions.

Inductions

Inductions occur twice a year. The process includes registration, an aptitude assessment, group interactions, and structured observation. Selected candidates are introduced to the functioning framework of Sankalp and assigned to Wings based on interest and merit.
